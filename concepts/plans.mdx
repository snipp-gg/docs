## Plan tiers

| Plan | Max file size | Upload limit |
|------|---------------|--------------|
| Free | 100 MB | 8 uploads/second |
| Plus | 5 GB | 8 uploads/second |
| Enterprise | 10 GB | 8 uploads/second |

## Plan features

| Feature | Free | Plus | Enterprise |
|---------|------|------|------------|
| File size limit | 100 MB | 5 GB | 10 GB |
| Discover feed ads | Yes | No | No |
| API access | Yes | Yes | Yes |

## Checking your plan

Call `GET /users/@me` to see your plan status. The response includes:

- `plus` — `true` if you have PLUS
- `enterprise` — `true` if you have Enterprise
- `uploads` — total number of uploads
