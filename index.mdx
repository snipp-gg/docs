## Three steps, zero hassle

From upload to share in seconds — it's that simple.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload">
    Drag & drop, paste, or use the API to upload your images and videos. Supports PNG, JPG, WebP, GIF, MP4, MOV, and MKV.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get a link">
    Instantly receive a shareable URL. No waiting, no processing delays.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share anywhere">
    Paste the link on Discord, X, or anywhere else. Your content loads fast on any platform.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## API features

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card
    title="Upload media"
    icon="cloud-arrow-up"
    href="/api-reference/introduction"
  >
    Upload images and videos via `POST /upload`. Up to 100 MB free, 5 GB with Plus.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Manage uploads"
    icon="list"
    href="/api-reference/introduction"
  >
    List and delete your uploads programmatically with `GET /uploads` and `DELETE /deleteUpload`.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="User data"
    icon="user"
    href="/api-reference/introduction"
  >
    Fetch your profile, upload count, and plan status with `GET /users/@me`.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Discover feed"
    icon="compass"
    href="/api-reference/introduction"
  >
    Access public uploads for the discover feed with `GET /discover`.
  </Card>
</Columns>
